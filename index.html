About Me

Hi! I am Lucas Daniel Romero, a technical-scientific translator in the English language. I graduated from Instituto Superior del Traductorado in La Plata, Argentina, where I completed all my courses with excellent grades. I am passionate about languages and helping bridge communication gaps through precise and professional translations.

Services Offered

I offer the following translation services:

  • Technical Translation
  • Scientific Translation
  • Executive Translation (different from "Legal Translation", a service I do not offer, for the moment)

Education and Certification

I hold a degree in Technical-Scientific Translation in the English Language from the Instituto Superior del Traductorado, certified by Resolution No. 293/10. This certification underscores my expertise in handling complex translations in technical and scientific domains, ensuring accuracy and clarity in both English and Spanish.
